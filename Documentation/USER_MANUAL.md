## System Overview

The Icons of IO Awards System is a comprehensive Power Apps solution designed to streamline the entire awards process from nomination to final selection. The system consists of five main components:

- **Nomination App**: For submitting award nominations
- **Admin Dashboard**: For managing the awards process
- **Reviewer Interface**: For reviewing and scoring nominations
- **Final Selection App**: For leadership to make final decisions
- **Reporting Dashboard**: For analytics and reporting

### User Roles

- **Nominators**: Employees who submit nominations
- **Reviewers**: Designated staff who evaluate nominations
- **Administrators**: Awards process managers
- **Leadership**: Senior staff who make final selections
- **System Admins**: Technical administrators

## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

1. **Navigate to Power Apps**: Go to [make.powerapps.com](https://make.powerapps.com)
2. **Sign in**: Use your corporate Microsoft 365 account
3. **Select Environment**: Choose the "Icons of IO Awards" environment
4. **Launch App**: Click on the appropriate app based on your role

### First-Time Setup

1. **Verify Profile**: Ensure your profile information is correct
2. **Check Permissions**: Confirm you can access the features you need
3. **Review Categories**: Familiarize yourself with available award categories

## Nomination Submission

### Starting a New Nomination

1. **Open Nomination App**
2. **Click "Start New Nomination"**
3. **Select Category**: Choose the appropriate award category
4. **Choose Nomination Type**: Individual or Team nomination

### Completing the Nomination Form

#### Nominee Information
- **Name**: Full name of the nominee(s)
- **Department**: Nominee's department/division
- **Role/Title**: Current position
- **Email**: Contact information
- **Manager**: Direct supervisor (auto-populated)

#### Nominator Information
- **Your Name**: Auto-populated from your profile
- **Department**: Your department
- **Relationship**: Your relationship to the nominee
- **Contact**: Your email and phone

#### Nomination Details
- **Reason for Nomination**: Clear, compelling reason (500 words max)
- **Key Achievements**: Specific accomplishments (300 words max)
- **Impact**: How the nominee's work benefited the organization
- **Supporting Evidence**: Examples, metrics, testimonials

#### Dynamic Fields
Based on the selected category, additional fields may appear:
- **Innovation**: Description of innovative solution
- **Leadership**: Examples of leadership impact
- **Collaboration**: Team achievements and collaboration examples
- **Customer Focus**: Customer satisfaction improvements

#### Attachments (Optional)
- **File Types**: PDF, Word documents, images
- **Size Limit**: 10MB per file
- **Examples**: Performance reviews, customer feedback, project documentation

### Submitting Your Nomination

1. **Review Information**: Check all fields for accuracy
2. **Preview**: Use the preview screen to see how your nomination appears
3. **Submit**: Click "Submit Nomination"
4. **Confirmation**: You'll receive a confirmation with reference number

### After Submission

- **Reference Number**: Save your confirmation number
- **Status Tracking**: Monitor progress through the system
- **Modifications**: Contact administrators for any changes needed

## Admin Dashboard

### Dashboard Overview

The main dashboard provides:
- **Total Nominations**: Current count of all submissions
- **Status Breakdown**: Pending, under review, completed
- **Category Distribution**: Nominations by award category
- **Review Progress**: Completion status by reviewer

### Managing Nominations

#### Viewing Nominations
1. **Navigate to Nominations Screen**
2. **Use Filters**: Filter by category, status, date, or reviewer
3. **Search**: Use the search bar for specific nominees or keywords
4. **Sort**: Click column headers to sort data

#### Scoring Nominations
1. **Select Nomination**: Click on a nomination to open details
2. **Review Content**: Read through all nomination information
3. **Add Score**: Rate on 1-5 scale (5 being highest)
4. **Add Comments**: Provide feedback and rationale
5. **Save**: Click "Save Score" to record your evaluation

#### Assigning Reviewers
1. **Select Nominations**: Use checkboxes to select multiple nominations
2. **Bulk Actions**: Click "Assign Reviewers"
3. **Choose Reviewers**: Select from available reviewer list
4. **Confirm Assignment**: Click "Assign" to notify reviewers

#### Managing Status
- **Update Status**: Change nomination status as it progresses
- **Add Notes**: Include internal comments for tracking
- **Set Deadlines**: Establish review deadlines

### Reviewer Management

#### Adding Reviewers
1. **Go to Reviewers Screen**
2. **Click "Add Reviewer"**
3. **Enter Information**:
   - Name and email
   - Department and role
   - Specialization areas
   - Maximum assignments
4. **Save Reviewer**

#### Managing Assignments
- **View Workload**: See current assignments per reviewer
- **Balance Load**: Redistribute assignments as needed
- **Track Progress**: Monitor completion rates

## Reviewer Interface

### Reviewer Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Assigned Nominations**: Total assigned to you
- **Completed Reviews**: Number you've finished
- **Pending Reviews**: Outstanding assignments
- **Average Score**: Your scoring average
- **Deadlines**: Upcoming review deadlines

### Reviewing Nominations

#### Accessing Assigned Nominations
1. **View Assignments**: See all nominations assigned to you
2. **Filter Options**: Filter by category, urgency, or status
3. **Sort by Priority**: Organize by deadline or importance

#### Conducting Reviews
1. **Open Nomination**: Click to view full details
2. **Read Thoroughly**: Review all provided information
3. **Consider Criteria**: Evaluate against award criteria
4. **Score Fairly**: Use the 1-5 scoring scale consistently
5. **Provide Feedback**: Add constructive comments

#### Scoring Guidelines
- **5 - Exceptional**: Outstanding achievement, clear winner
- **4 - Excellent**: Strong candidate, exceeds expectations
- **3 - Good**: Solid nomination, meets criteria well
- **2 - Fair**: Adequate but not compelling
- **1 - Poor**: Does not meet award criteria

#### Adding Comments
- **Be Specific**: Provide detailed feedback
- **Be Constructive**: Focus on achievements and impact
- **Be Professional**: Maintain respectful tone
- **Include Rationale**: Explain your scoring decision

### Completing Reviews

1. **Final Review**: Double-check your score and comments
2. **Submit Review**: Click "Submit Review" to finalize
3. **Confirmation**: System confirms successful submission
4. **Status Update**: Nomination status updates automatically

### Managing Your Workload

- **Track Progress**: Monitor completion percentage
- **Prioritize**: Focus on urgent or high-priority nominations
- **Request Extensions**: Contact administrators if needed
- **Quality Focus**: Maintain thorough, fair evaluations

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Cannot Access Application
**Symptoms**: Error message when opening app
**Solutions**:
1. Verify you're signed in with correct account
2. Check if you're in the right environment
3. Confirm you have necessary permissions
4. Clear browser cache and try again

#### Nomination Won't Submit
**Symptoms**: Submit button doesn't work or shows error
**Solutions**:
1. Check all required fields are completed
2. Verify file attachments are within size limits
3. Ensure text fields don't exceed word limits
4. Try refreshing the page and resubmitting

#### Can't See Assigned Nominations
**Symptoms**: Reviewer interface shows no assignments
**Solutions**:
1. Confirm you're added as a reviewer in the system
2. Check if nominations have been assigned to you
3. Verify you're looking in the correct category
4. Contact administrator to verify assignments

#### Scores Not Saving
**Symptoms**: Scores disappear after entering
**Solutions**:
1. Ensure you click "Save" after entering scores
2. Check your internet connection
3. Verify you have edit permissions
4. Try refreshing and re-entering scores

#### Reports Not Loading
**Symptoms**: Dashboard shows no data or errors
**Solutions**:
1. Check if you have reporting permissions
2. Verify data exists for selected filters
3. Try clearing filters and reloading
4. Contact system administrator

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Who can submit nominations?**
A: All employees can submit nominations for their colleagues, teams, or departments.

**Q: Can I nominate myself?**
A: Self-nominations are typically not allowed, but check with your Awards Committee for specific rules.

**Q: How many nominations can I submit?**
A: There's usually no limit, but focus on quality over quantity.

**Q: Can I edit my nomination after submission?**
A: No, nominations cannot be edited after submission. Contact an administrator if changes are needed.

### Nomination Process

**Q: What makes a strong nomination?**
A: Specific examples, measurable impact, and clear connection to award criteria.

**Q: How long should my nomination be?**
A: Follow the word limits provided (typically 300-500 words per section).

**Q: What supporting documents should I include?**
A: Performance reviews, customer feedback, project results, or other relevant evidence.

**Q: When is the nomination deadline?**
A: Check with your Awards Committee for specific dates, typically announced annually.

### Review Process

**Q: How are reviewers selected?**
A: Reviewers are chosen based on expertise, role, and availability by the Awards Committee.

**Q: How long do I have to complete reviews?**
A: Review deadlines are set by administrators, typically 2-3 weeks.

**Q: Can I see other reviewers' scores?**
A: No, reviews are confidential until the process is complete.

**Q: What if I have a conflict of interest?**
A: Notify administrators immediately to reassign the nomination.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Microsoft Edge, Chrome, Firefox, and Safari (latest versions).

**Q: Can I use the system on mobile devices?**
A: Yes, the apps are responsive and work on tablets and smartphones.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, the system uses Microsoft 365 security and follows company data policies.

**Q: How long is data retained?**
A: Typically 7 years for audit purposes, but check your organization's policy.

### Results and Awards

**Q: When will winners be announced?**
A: Announcement dates are set by the Awards Committee, usually after final selection.

**Q: Will I be notified of results?**
A: Yes, nominators and nominees typically receive notification of outcomes.

**Q: Can I appeal a decision?**
A: Appeal processes vary by organization; check with your Awards Committee.

**Q: Are runner-up recognitions given?**
A: This depends on your organization's awards program structure.
